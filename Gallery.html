about Smkfit

Shree Motilal Kanhaiyalal Fomra Institute of Technology, also known as SMKFIT is located in the Thaiyur Village near Kelambakkam on Old Mahabalipuram Road (IT Highway) 35km from Chennai, is spread over a sprawling area of 25 acres. It is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Established in 2001, the college aims to impart education that thoroughly prepares students for a professional career in any field of their choice. The college imparts courses in varios branches of engineering levels. The courses are conducted as per the guidelines of AICTE.
